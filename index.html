FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers to help you get the most out of Fitmonks AI.

How does the AI Fitness Coach customize my routines?

Our context-aware AI engine reads your height, current weight, activity tier, and past logged sessions to suggest recovery options or scale daily macros automatically.

Can I track sets in pounds instead of kilograms?

Yes! Under the Profile/Settings panel, you can adjust units, target calories, age metrics, and water levels to fit your active system parameters.

Is there an offline mode for workout logging?

Yes! All workout sets and weights are cached inside local state storage instantly. Your data is persisted, allowing offline tracking anywhere.
